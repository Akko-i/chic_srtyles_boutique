Privacy Policy

At Chic Styles Boutique, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and transparently. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of personal data:
・Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing addresses, and payment details to process your orders and communicate with you.
・Account Information: Details you provide when creating an account on our website.
・Usage Data: Information such as IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
・Process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and returns.
・Send you updates about your orders, promotions, and new arrivals (with your consent).
・Improve our website, services, and customer experience.
・Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. How We Protect Your Data
Your personal information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. We use encryption and secure payment gateways to protect sensitive data during transactions.

4. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners, such as:
・Payment processors to complete transactions.
・Shipping companies to deliver your orders.
・Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Your Rights
You have the right to:
・Access and review your personal information.
・Update or correct inaccuracies in your data.
・Request the deletion of your information, where applicable.
・Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Cookies and Tracking
We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies if you prefer.

7. Contact Us
If you have questions about our Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:
・Email: support@chicstylesboutique.com
・Phone: +61 400 000 000

8. Updates to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please review it regularly.
